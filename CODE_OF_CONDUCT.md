# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

BenchForge is an open, welcoming project. Everyone who participates — whether contributing code, documentation, issues, or discussion — is expected to treat others with respect.

## Our Standards

Behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- being respectful and constructive in feedback
- welcoming contributors of different experience levels
- focusing on the problem, not the person
- accepting constructive criticism gracefully

Behavior that is not acceptable:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- publishing others' private information without consent
- sustained disruptive behavior in discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer via GitHub. See the maintainer's [GitHub profile](https://github.com/tlucekP) for contact options.

Reports will be reviewed and responded to. The maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions and to temporarily or permanently ban anyone whose behavior is harmful to the project.
